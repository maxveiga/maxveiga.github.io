Introducing the Amphicar!

This is the Amphicar 770. It was made between the years of 1961-1967 designed by a German engineer by the name of Hanns Trippel. Inspired by the Volkswagen Schwimmwagen from WWII, its amphibious capabilities allow it to move at an upwards of 7 knots in the water and 70mph on land giving it the model name 770.



Amphicars at the annual International Amphicar Owners Club swim-in in Celina, Ohio

With a total of 3,878 manufactured, it makes this car one of the most rare and proudly collected vehicles available.
